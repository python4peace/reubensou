About Reuben

Reuben Gobah, known to the world as Reuben Soul, was honored as the World Peace and Fitness Ambassador in Los Angeles in 2023 for his extraordinary contribution to global health and unity.

He has helped thousands through major gyms like 24 Hour Fitness, Equinox, Bally Total Fitness (now LA Fitness), and private coaching—especially during COVID. He runs three marathons every year for peace, works afternoons at Costco (where he also plays trumpet and sings to uplift members), and performs at night. All in a single day. All for unity.
